## Quick Facts

Stemming originally from a more temperate part of the Teholitlan continent, the Pandoid species has spread around the world. They are often regarded as strong brutes due to their unusually high stature and muscle mass, which makes them sought after on many ships built by the larger species. Seeing the pandoids as one homogenous group, adept at menial labour and nothing more, would be a disservice though. While it is true that the black Pandoids are biologically inclined to build massive muscles they are also quite capable of craftsmanship and magic.
