## Religions


### Nogrian Standard Pantheon

1. Ulthra: The Mother, main goddess and Mother to all other goddesses an lifeforms.
	• Represents stability and stagnation
	• Her tenants are: Compassion, Friendship, Courtship, Love, Tenderness, Growth
2. Wodenia: The Huntress, goddess of the forest and its animals.
	• Represents ingenuity and absent mindedness
	• Her tenants are: Athleticism, Altruism, Open mindedness, Respectfulness to life, Resolute-
ness, Intelligence
3. Elfharda: The Admiral, goddess of the sea and patron deity of seafarers and fishermen.
	• Represents adventurism and recklessness
	• Her tenants are: Fearlessness, Compassion, Bravery, Wanderlust, Ambition, Ingenuity
4. Hadre: The Collector, goddess of death and decay, patron deity to soldiers and murderers.
	• Represents honour and courage as well as carnage and chaos
	• Her tenants are: Honour, Righteousness, Bravery, Fearlessness, Resoluteness, Intelligence
5. Angroa: The Explorer, goddess of wisdom, patron deity to scholars and explorers.
	• Represents flexibility and strife
	• Her tenants are: Curiosity, Friendship, Intelligence, Cooperation, Compassion, Ambition
6. Guthed: The Charlatan, goddess of commerce and lies, patron of cities and their citizens.
	• Represents Trust and Deceit
	• Her tenants are: Compassion, Courtship, Friendship, Ambition, Avarice, Intelligence


### Followers of Althea

The [Altheans](../Religions/Sects/Altheans.md) have [Althea Homilion](../Legendary%20People/Nogr/Althea%20Homilion.md) as their main goddess after her ascension following the crisis in [Angamarum](../Cities/Nogr/Angamarum.md). Their belief is structured after the life which their goddess supposedly lived. Due to Althea's widely known bouts of nudity her followers have decided to adopt this stance as a core tenant of their belief, forsaking any and all clothing unless absolutely necessary. As necessary deemed clothes are backpacks, blankets and medical coverings. Secondly they believe in helping anyone in need. They are most commonly known as missionaries or care workers, doing their best to help the lost and forgotten of society live a better life. This is coincidentally where they find most of their converts, making the Altheans a powerful sect in the lower levels of society. 


### The Great Dragons 

The followers of the Great Dragons believe in the idea that either one (called monolists) or two (called dualists) dragons once created the known world. It places great importance on the sanctity of dragon kind and is fundamental to [Turian](../Continents/Ma'antur.md) society. Due to this belief a great many people suspect that Ma'antur is host to a high number of dragons but neither Turian priesthood nor the populace itself claim to have seen any dragons around their cities. 


### The Litan Pantheon

In [Litan theology](../Religions/Teholitlan/Three%20Gods%20Pantheon.md) there is the belief that there once were many great and powerful gods which lived together. Since they had such power, they had nothing to wish for and spent their existence in revelry and debauchery. The three gods: Quetzal, Qotal and Qelzotal saw the weakness of their siblings and decided to step in. Disgusted by what they saw they chose to slaughter their siblings to the last. Supposedly their blood was the base from which were created the liquids of Tem-Bel. Their meat would become the soil upon which new life would grow. And their bones would become the rock and metal, which permeated said soil. From said gods do the inhabitants of [Teholitlan](../Continents/Teholitlan.md), the so called [Eholit](../Species/Sentient/Lizardfolk.md) trace their lineage. Firmly rooted in Litan society, the belief of the three gods has not yet found much purchase outside Teholitlan proper. To be a believer in the Three gods, is to constantly face challenges. A warrior may follow the teachings of Quetzal and seek to become the greatest warrior in history. An artisan might feel more inclined towards Qotal and their penchant for craftsmanship. And an administrator might look to Qelzotal's unmatched wisdom and knowledge in all matters scientific. Regardless of one's personal following each disciple of the three gods will from the moment they are born or converted seek with endless ambition to be the best at what they do. This has made Teholitlan a hotbed for the finest craftsmanship on that side of the globe, as well as the place to seek bodyguards or assassins, or to just listen to traditional Litan symphonies.
