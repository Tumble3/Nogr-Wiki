## Kobolds

Are warm blooded creatures from Ma'antur.  
They eat very little but are usually finely attuned to magic and very clever.  
They usually occupy the political and religious roles in their home country.  
Are almost uniformly believers in the Great Dragons religion.  
Their priests are rumoured to frequently consort with dragons, which supposedly is one reason for their rarely deviating faith.
